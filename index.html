He was a poet, devotee, philosopher, logician and master-teacher (desikan). He was a disciple of Kidambi Appullar, also known as Aathreya Ramanujachariar, who himself was of a master-disciple lineage that began with Ramanuja. Swami Vedanta Desika is considered to be the avatar (incarnation) of the divine bell of Venkateswara of Tirumalai.
